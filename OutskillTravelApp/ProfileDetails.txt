#### 1. **Eco-Planner**

Calm, conscientious travelers who care deeply about impact and like control.

Profile:
They want lower-carbon routes, are willing to accept longer travel times if framed as “eco” and “thoughtful,” and like detailed plans, clear expectations, and trustworthy providers.

Use this profile for:
- Train over flight, direct over layovers if emissions differ, night trains.  
- Detailed, low-impact itineraries; carbon labels; local, certified stays.  
- Messaging: “Thoughtfully planned,” “lower impact,” “support local communities.”

---

#### 2. **Eco-Adventurer**

Environmentally conscious, but also high on novelty and risk.

Profile:
They want meaningful, lower-impact trips with some edge: hiking, biking, remote eco-lodges, volunteering, or “off the beaten path” but still sustainable.

Use this profile for:
- Adventure routes with lower emissions (trekking, cycling, sailing instead of short flights).  
- Eco-lodges, community-based tourism, wildlife & nature trips.  
- Messaging: “Adventure with purpose,” “off-grid but low impact.”

---

#### 3. **Spontaneous Adventurer**

Thrill-seeking, novelty-oriented, flexible about plans, less driven by impact.

Profile:
They want freedom, last-minute options, and “cool things to do now.” Less interested in detailed itineraries; more in surf trips, night-life, extreme sports, remote exploration.

Use this profile for:
- Flexible tickets, last-minute deals, open-jaw or one-way suggestions.  
- Adventure activities, adrenaline sports, “hidden gems.”  
- Messaging: “Go with the flow,” “book now, decide later,” “spontaneous getaways.”

---

#### 4. **Comfort-Seeking Relaxer**

Low on risk/novelty, high on comfort and predictability.

Profile:
They want easy, safe, comfortable experiences: resorts, all-inclusives, familiar destinations, smooth transfers, and minimal hassle.

Use this profile for:
- Direct routes, high comfort class when possible, fewer transfers.  
- Beach resorts, spa retreats, cruises, quiet stays.  
- Messaging: “Stress-free,” “all taken care of,” “relax and unwind.”

---

#### 5. **Cultural Explorer**

Curious, reflective travelers who want depth over adrenaline.


Profile:
They want local food, history, art, walking tours, homestays, language experiences; okay with some complexity if it leads to authenticity.

Use this profile for:
- City trips, cultural routes, heritage sites, local guides, cooking classes.  
- Slow travel options (trains, multi-day stays in one city).  
- Messaging: “Live like a local,” “learn, connect, understand.”

---

#### 6. **Community-Focused Changemaker**

Strong value-driven travelers; impact and local benefit are central.


Profile:
They want their money to support local people, not just big chains. They’re open to homestays, co-ops, volunteering, social enterprises, and responsible wildlife or conservation projects.

Use this profile for:
- Community-based tourism, homestays, farm stays, volunteering.  
- Clear information on where money goes, certifications, impact metrics.  
- Messaging: “Travel that gives back,” “support local livelihoods,” “ethical experiences.”

---

#### 7. **Social Weekender**

Moderate novelty, very social, often comfort or light adventure focused.

Profile:
They want fun, social, often short trips: city breaks, festivals, nightlife, group tours.

Use this profile for:
- Group tours, hostels, social hostels, co-living, festivals.  
- Short city trips with nightlife, food, and events.  
- Messaging: “Meet new people,” “group fun,” “shared experiences.”

---

#### 8. **Solo Wanderer**

More introspective; values autonomy, may be structured or freeform.

Profile:
They value independence, reflection, and control over their schedule. May lean to quiet nature, small towns, or slower city exploration.

Use this profile for:
- Safe solo-friendly destinations and routes.  
- Clear logistics and good information, especially for Solo Planner; open tickets and flexible stays for Solo Drifter.  
- Messaging: “Take time for yourself,” “go at your own pace.”
